Game Overview


I scripted modular a dungeon generator that works with any width or height

• The dungeon generator works with any width or height.
• The dungeon will never loop
• Assets are placed using an marching squares algorithm, supporting corners
• Assets can easily be replaced
• Player can move around using A*, Dijkstra and NavMesh
• Dungeon generation and the player’s generated path are clearly shown with gizmos

Game & Development


The development of this dungeon generator taught me a lot about the use and existence of algorithms like flood fill, marching squares, A*, Dijkstra, DFS and BFS.

What Did I learn?


• Improved my understanding of OOP concepts like polymorphism, Encapsulation, and inheritance. The development also taught me about the importance of modular and reusable code

• Learned the do, don’ts and limits of Unity animation system.

• Focused and big usage of modular and easily reusable code.

• Gained a lot of solo dev experience, from making the assets to bringing them to life in unity.
